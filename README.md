# TechBot-T1
A more affordable range of LoRa transceiver and repeaters and stuff.

The TechBot-T1 is meant to be a more rugged, durable and longer-lasting meshtastic device. 

TechBot-T1 is currently estimated to have 3 days of battery life at full load of constantly receving and processing data and the OLED is always on and LEDs are lit.

Typically with the device sleeping most of the time and waking every 60 secs, turning the OLED on for 5 seconds, transmitting a LoRa packet and going back to sleep, it is estimated to last ~87 days

In a example use case, the device is always on with the OLED off, but Bluetooth is active to pair with the phone, and broadcast location (GPS from phone) every 15 mins. Estimated to last ~43 days in ideal conditions or ~7 to 10 days in cold alpine conditions.
